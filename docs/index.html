Why DialectOS?

🎯 Dialect-Aware

Understands 20 Spanish regional variants — not just "Spanish". Mexico, Argentina, Colombia, Spain, and more.

🛡️ Structure-Preserving

Tables, code blocks, links, and frontmatter stay intact during translation. No broken markdown.

🤖 MCP Native

16 tools that integrate directly with Claude, Cursor, and any MCP client. No wrappers needed.

📊 Quality Gates

Semantic drift detection, token integrity, glossary fidelity, and structure validation catch errors before release.

🔒 Security-Hardened

SSRF protection, circuit breakers, rate limiting, and adversarial testing. Zero audit vulnerabilities.

🔧 Self-Hostable

DeepL for quality, LibreTranslate for privacy, MyMemory for free tier. Choose your tradeoff.
